0. Backend napojenie & kontext

  • Storefront: Shopify = cielovy standard; odchod od stareho Magenta.
  • Multi-market: e-shop postavit ako multi-market (Shopify Markets) — nielen CZ, ale pripravene na dalsie trhy/jazyky (SK + expanzia podla domenoveho portfolia).
  • ERP/ucto: Pohoda (entita Vitar Digital) — funkcne napojenie cez Dativery (alebo vlastne napojenie): objednavky, faktury, sklad, parovanie platieb.
  • WMS / DC: samostatny prud — launch e-shopu od neho nezavisi.
Pravidlo: kazda objednavka pada do jednej entity (Pohoda) → jedno WMS → jedno DC.

4. PIM atribucny model (22 poli)

P0 (must pred launchom): master_key, sku, ean, sbu_owner, brand_architecture_role, category, need_state, active_ingredients, form, dosage, target_group, claims, warnings, price, availability, canonical_url.

P1: pack_size, flavour, age_group, quality_proof, cross_sell_matrix, structured_data (Product/Offer/Brand/Breadcrumb/FAQ).

Owneri: Identity/Product facts = PIM/Data; need_state/category = E-commerce; claims/warnings/dosage = Regulatory/Legal (overclaiming!); price/availability z nasevitaminy.cz / novej platformy (Vitar.cz casto nema commerce cenu).

5. Funkcny rozsah

Must-have (Vitar.cz)

  • Katalog + parametricke filtre cez metafields (P0 filtre + SEO indexacia).
  • Produktova karta: varianty (thumbnail swatche), nutricne tabulky + davkovanie, claims/warnings, recenzie.
  • Landing pages: brand (16), category/need-state (~15) + kurátorovane ingredient/segment landingy.
  • Bundly (Shopify native) + cross-sell/upsell.
  • Konfigurator / dotaznik (person profily → personalizovane bundly) — reuse z existujucej bazy.
  • Zlavy + anti-kanibalizacia (Vitar.cz premium nesmie podstrelovat retail).
  • B2B (ak relevantne): Shopify B2B; pre velkoobchod/export zahrnut exportny team.

nasevitaminy.cz: redukovany set — jednoduchy katalog (klasiky), vypredajove mechaniky, minimalny brand layer.
